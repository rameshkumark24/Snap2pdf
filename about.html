About Snap2PDF

Snap2PDF is a free, lightweight web app that lets you instantly capture photos from your camera or upload files, and turn them into professional PDFs — right in your browser. No sign-in. No upload. No storage. Just work → download → done.


Everything runs 100% locally on your device using open-source JavaScript libraries like jsPDF, pdf-lib, and fabric.js. This means your data never leaves your computer — ensuring total privacy and security.

Features

  • 📸 Convert camera images directly to PDF
  • 📂 Merge multiple PDFs instantly
  • 📝 Edit and add text to existing PDFs
  • 🔒 100% offline and secure (no data upload)
  • ⚡ Fast and mobile-friendly

Our Vision

We built Snap2PDF to make document conversion and editing simple, private, and free forever. While big tools hide features behind sign-ups or subscriptions, Snap2PDF gives you a zero-login, zero-cost experience.
