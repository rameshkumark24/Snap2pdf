About Snap2PDF

Snap2PDF is a free, privacy-focused web app that lets you perform essential PDF tasks right in your browser. Capture photos, merge files, extract text, and more—all without ever uploading your data to a server. Everything runs 100% locally on your device.

Core Features

  • 📸 Camera to PDF: Instantly convert camera snapshots to PDF.
  • ➕ Merge PDFs: Combine multiple PDF documents into one.
  • ✂️ Split PDF: Extract a specific page from any PDF file.
  • 📄 PDF to Text: Pull all text content from a PDF for easy copying.
  • 📝 Inline Editor: Add text annotations directly onto a PDF page.
  • 🔒 100% Offline & Secure: Your files are never uploaded, ensuring total privacy.

Our Vision

We believe powerful tools should be simple, accessible, and private. Snap2PDF was built to provide a zero-login, zero-cost, and zero-compromise experience for your daily document needs. We rely on powerful open-source libraries like jsPDF, pdf-lib, and Fabric.js.
